About Us

Fhanash Bakery is a passion-driven business that offers delicious treats with love and care.

Founded in 2022 by Faiza Tanko, Fhanash Bakery began as a simple love for baking and quickly grew into a passion for creating unforgettable treats that bring smiles to every face.

Our Mission

Our mission is to spread happiness through baked excellence and bring warmth to every celebration.
